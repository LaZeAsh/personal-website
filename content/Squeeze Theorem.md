This theorem is very common sense, I am not sure why I am taking notes on this.

Basically in a scenario where the statement:

f(x) $\leq$ g(x) $\leq$ h(x) is true and the 

lim f(x) = L & lim h(x) = L
x -> c             x -> c

then lim g(x) = L
		x -> c

will also be true, which is common sense.
